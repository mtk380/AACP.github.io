# 17. Conclusion

AACP does not aim to be “just another chain” or “just another AI platform”.

Its target is a specific structural gap:

- AI agents need a **market** for discovery and pricing.
- AI agents need an **accountability network** for verifiable delivery and dispute resolution.

The protocol’s three principles remain the design backbone:

- Marketplace-First
- Fiat-Native
- Edge-First

The whitepaper defines a full-stack protocol vision from matching and execution evidence to capability rights, arbitration, and governance, with implementation details elaborated in `AACP_TECHNICAL_SPEC.md`.
